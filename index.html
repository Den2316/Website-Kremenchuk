Architecture

Saint Nicholaus Church

Built in 1910, times when Ukraine was a part of Russian Empire, the Church became one of the symbols of Kremenchuk. In Soviet times Church was never used for praying, but only used for administrational aims. Since in 1991 the USSR has broken down, activity in the Church came back to its original purposes and nowadays it functions as one of the churches of Ukrainian Orthodoxy.

Kryukiv Bridge

First bridge on Dnipro river was built in 1879, however it was destroyed during WW2. In 1949 a new, Kryukiv Bridge, was constructed.It has length of 1.7 kilometers, supports both car, railway traffic, and has an option to be lifted in case of massive ferries wanting to drive through it. Nowadays this bridge is the only connection between two banks of Kremenchuk, however there are plans to build new bridges nearby.

Governmental Bank

Governmental Bank was built in 1900 and designed to be an official bank of Russian Empire. What's interesting about this place, is that it was one of not so many old buildings in Kremenchuk to survive WW2, avoid any harmful modifications since 1900 and still stand in the centre of the city.

Food review

Celentano restaurant
The best of the best to taste good old Italian cuizine. We strongly recommend this place.
4 seasons restaurant
Not the cheapest restaurant, but here you can afford having a great business lunch in a daytime, and pleasant dinner in the evening.
Levee restaurant
It is not situated inside of Kremenchuk, but it is not far from our city. In our opinion, the best restaurant among all in Kremenchuk region.Located on the bank of Kremenchuk Sea, absolutely worth visiting.
Kofan
A cheap, comfortable,peaceful cafe in the center of the city. A best place to drink coffee and eat waffles. On Friday's evenings a live music is performed.
Hutirec
You can taste national Ukrainian cuizine here. Must visit for those, who wish to eat a lot of different Ukrainian dishes.
New York Street Pizza restaurant
If you didn't like Celentano, or just wish to diversify your meal, visit this restaurant and eat some pizza here!

Famous people in Kremenchuk

Our city is full of famous people, whose lives were connected with Kremenchuk. In different times we had such events related to famous people:
  • Kremenchuk was visited by Kotlyarevskyi, Lysenko, Gogol, Pushkin, Kateryna II
  • Taras Shevchenko visited our city and wrote a poem about it
  • Kremenchuk was a place to work in for Vernadskyi, Utyosov, Maiboroda
